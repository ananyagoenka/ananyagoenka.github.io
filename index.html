Feature Detection

The objective is to develop code that can identify distinctive features in an image, which remain relatively unchanged despite translation, rotation, and illumination variations. These discerning features will then be utilized to establish correspondences with the most suitable matching features in another image. By achieving this, you will enable the stitching of multiple images together, ultimately creating a panoramic view.

Computational Neural Network

In my exploration of image classification, I delved into the realm of convolutional neural networks (CNNs). To begin, I meticulously constructed and trained a baseline model. With the goal of improving its performance, I proceeded to implement various data augmentation techniques. Taking it a step further, I crafted a customized model from scratch, aiming to surpass the capabilities of the baseline. To comprehensively understand the strengths and weaknesses of CNNs, I also generated adversarial examples, allowing me to scrutinize any potential shortcomings. This systematic approach provided invaluable insights into the application of CNNs in image classification tasks, furthering my understanding of this intricate field.
